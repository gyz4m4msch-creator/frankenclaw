Infrastructure that outlasts any model.
Your knowledge, your workflows, your decisions.

FRANK is a private AI engine that runs on your Mac and serves your entire ecosystem — family office, investment entities, real estate, and operating companies. It ingests documents from any source (email, Dropbox, iMessage, WhatsApp), learns each entity's unique knowledge, routes requests to specialist agents (finance, legal, research), and requires your approval before any action or transaction. No cloud lock‑in. No autonomous spending. Just a tireless, auditable chief of staff for your capital and affairs.
