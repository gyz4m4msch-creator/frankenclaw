Built for complexity

Every entity.
A first‑class citizen.

A family office. Several investment entities. Real estate holdings. Operating businesses. Each with its own documents, counterparties, and decision rights. FRANK keeps them distinct — and keeps you in command.

Drop in a term sheet.

FRANK classifies it, extracts the key terms, routes it to the finance agent, and drafts a summary. You approve or reject — and your decision, rationale included, is stored in that entity's memory.

Ask from anywhere.

"What's our exposure to European real estate?" FRANK retrieves from every relevant entity, synthesises a response, and waits for your approval before sending. No data crosses entity lines unless you explicitly permit it.

It learns the way you decide.

Your investment criteria. Your recurring due‑diligence questions. Your preferred legal language. FRANK becomes shared institutional memory — a governance layer that outlasts any single manager or model.
